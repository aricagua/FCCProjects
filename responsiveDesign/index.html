Naruto Uzumaki the
7th Hokage

A hard past forged is identity

He lost his parents on the day of his birth as they tried to protect him from the Kyubi (Nine-Tailed Fox). Showing the love they felt for him. As if that were not enough, his village (The Village of the Leaf) turned their back on him because they linked him to the tragedy of the day he was born. Forcing him to lead a lonely childhood.

He never gave up

Despite his life, he always had one goal in mind, to prove that he could be the best in the village. And to accomplish this he decided to train a lot.

Until becoming the 7th Hokage
